We offer what you are looking for!

Discover your holiday home at the edge to the forest, just over the city of Bad Urach. Here, you can enjoy a breathtaking view of the famous castle ruin from your terrace with a bbq. This is undoubtly the ideal starting point for hiking or mountainbiking tours, as well as shopping trips to the Outletcity Metzingen. The historical city center of Bad Urach can be reached within ten minutes by foot.

Erkunden, Erleben, Entspannen

We have collected various activities in and around the city of Bad Urach. Offering three train staions and great proximity to the most beautiful places of the Swabian Alb, Bad Urach is the ideal base for your holiday. Your holiday home offers activities for everyone: Shopping in one of Germany's largest outlentcenters in Metzingen, Hiking on the Albtrauf, exploring mystical caves, marvelous castles or ruines thereof. To conclude on a eventful day, Bad Urach offers numerous opportunities for relexation. To ease an overview for the countless choices for you, we have compiled a collection of offers.
